SFERA sub-applications

The basis of the SFERA PROJECT application is a messenger, on the basis of which versatile services already exist and are constantly being added, for example, a job search service (Job Light), a dating service (MeetNet), a friend search service (Find Friends), etc. All services are an integral part of SFERA and work as a single application. At the moment, all services are developed by us, but in the future it is planned to connect third-party services.

At the moment, the services of the first stage of development are available in the system.
